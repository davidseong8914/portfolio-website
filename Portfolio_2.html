Selected Projects

WYBot

My first quadruped robot created to study 8 DoF gait algorithms. Later added obstacle avoidance to enhance functionality.

Torsobot

A 12-pivot rimless wheel robot engineered to simulate the human walk cycle. Its lightweight, streamlined design enhances movement precision and stability, making it ideal for studying locomotive biomechanics across different terrains.

Learning for Powder Bed Fusion

Novel Powder Spreadability System for Powder Bed Fusion. Created as my senior design project, this device uses Pronterface - a 3D printing software to automatically adjust powder bed height, dump powder, spread powder and measure powder weight to determine powder bed density. Future work includes adding a camera, ML techniques to determine surface roughness from top view of the powder spread.
