T-Bone: Tail-Based Oscillatory Navigator Explorer

Inspired by the elegance of fish propulsion, T-Bone is a bio-inspired robotic swimmer with five modular tail segments and interchangeable fins. Its design lets us rapidly swap horizontal and vertical profiles to optimize thrust, stability, and maneuverability in water.
